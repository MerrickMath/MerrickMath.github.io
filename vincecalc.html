Exams

Unit 10 final unit test scheduled for Monday, March 24 (in class). Majority of questions focus on 10.10–10.15. You should be able to:

  • Determine convergence/divergence (partial sums, geometric, nth-term, p-series, comparison, alternating series, ratio, absolute convergence)
  • Apply the AST error bound
  • Find and use Taylor/Maclaurin polynomials; apply Lagrange error bound
  • Find radius/interval of convergence; build series via substitution/ops/diff/integration

Exam weighting: 17.5. Note: any material from Units 1–9 and 10.1–10.9 is fair game.
